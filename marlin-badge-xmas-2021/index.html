Marlin-renderer badges (2021.12)

This page presents the new badges to express your support or congratulations to the Marlin-renderer projects: marlin-renderer, marlin-fx, mapbench repositories on github.

I made this creative content myself on linux using Gimp 2.10, free fonts and my own-made marlin fish drawing (shown at JavaOne in 2017.09) painted by my children.
